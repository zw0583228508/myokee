You are a world-class product designer, UX strategist, and startup marketing writer.

Your task is to **analyze, redesign, and rewrite the entire website of an AI product called MYOUKEE**.

MYOUKEE is an AI karaoke platform that allows users to turn **any song into karaoke automatically**.

The system:
• separates vocals from music using AI
• transcribes lyrics automatically
• synchronizes lyrics to the song
• allows users to sing in real time
• scores their performance
• creates shareable karaoke videos

Users can upload a file or paste a YouTube link.

The first **40 seconds are free**.

Your job is to **improve the website in all aspects**:
• structure
• hierarchy
• clarity
• marketing copy
• UX flow
• section order
• readability
• emotional impact

The website must feel like a **premium AI startup product** comparable to Suno, ElevenLabs, or Midjourney.

---

Rewrite the website with a **clear and logical structure**:

1. HERO SECTION
   Create a powerful headline explaining the core value.

Focus on the key message:
“Turn any song into karaoke with AI”.

Add a short, exciting subheadline explaining the experience.

Include feature highlights such as:
• real-time singing
• global leaderboard
• karaoke battles
• party mode
• support for 100+ languages

Add two CTA buttons:
Primary: “Create Karaoke Now”
Secondary: “View Leaderboard”

Add supporting text:
“First 40 seconds free – no credit card required.”

---

2. PRODUCT VALUE SECTION
   Explain clearly what MYOUKEE does in simple language.

Highlight the unique advantage:
“No karaoke library required. Any song works.”

Explain that users can upload a file or paste a YouTube link.

---

3. HOW IT WORKS
   Create a simple three-step process.

Step 1 – Upload a song
Step 2 – AI processes the audio
Step 3 – Sing, compete, and share

Use very short descriptions.

---

4. CORE FEATURES
   Create a clean feature grid with short descriptions.

Features include:
• real-time pitch detection
• global leaderboard
• battle mode (challenge friends)
• party mode for karaoke events
• automatic viral clip generation
• MP4 karaoke video export

Keep each description short and engaging.

---

5. AI TECHNOLOGY
   Explain the AI stack in simple language.

Demucs AI – separates vocals from music
Whisper AI – transcribes and synchronizes lyrics

Mention that MYOUKEE supports **100+ languages**.

---

6. SOCIAL EXPERIENCE
   Explain the competitive and social aspects of the platform.

Include:
• leaderboards
• friend challenges
• weekly karaoke challenges
• performance ratings and stars

Make this section exciting and gamified.

---

7. WHY MYOUKEE
   Explain what makes the platform unique.

• No karaoke database required
• Works with any song
• Real-time singing analysis
• AI-generated karaoke videos
• Built for sharing and competition

---

8. FINAL CALL TO ACTION
   Create a strong closing section encouraging users to start singing.

Example headline:
“Ready to turn your favorite song into karaoke?”

CTA button:
“Create Karaoke Now”

Supporting text:
“Upload a song or paste a YouTube link. First 40 seconds free.”

---

Design and writing guidelines:

• Use clear visual hierarchy
• Use large headings and short paragraphs
• Avoid repetition
• Keep the tone modern and exciting
• Write like a premium AI startup product
• Make the page easy to scan and visually structured
• Focus on user excitement and conversion

The final result should feel like a **high-end AI product landing page**.
