Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

MYOUKEE collects the following information:

  • Google account details (name, email address, profile picture) — for authentication only.
  • Audio and video files you upload for karaoke processing.
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, and anonymous usage data.
  • Performance scores you save to the leaderboard (name, score, song name).

2. Use of Information

We use the information to:

  • Provide the karaoke service — processing, vocal separation, and transcription.
  • Manage your credits balance.
  • Improve the system using anonymous data.
  • Enable your personal history gallery.

3. Storage and Security

Processed files are stored on a secure server. Files may be deleted 30 days after creation. We implement encryption in transit (TLS) and restrict data access.

4. Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Stripe — for payment processing only.
  • Google — for authentication.

5. Your Rights

In accordance with Israeli Privacy Protection Law and GDPR (for EU citizens), you have the right to:

  • Review the information collected about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.

For inquiries: windot100@gmail.com

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management. No advertising cookies are used.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may change. Material changes will be published on the website and a notification will be sent to the registered email address.
